How It Works

Trading Pieces is a puzzle subscription service. You remember in the early days of Netflix, when you paid a monthly price to ship DVDs to your house? Think of us as that but with puzzles! First you choose a plan based on how many puzzles you think you'll do.

Once you've got your plan, it's time to pick out your puzzles. We offer a wide variety of puzzle designs, sizes, brands, and more - so there's a puzzle out there for everyone! After your puzzles are selected, we'll ship them to you and then it's happy puzzling!

When your puzzle is complete, pack it up, ship it back, and select another. It's that easy! OR if you want to keep it, pay the value of the puzzle and it's yours! We also sell puzzle glue and framing supplies if that's your style too. With Trading Pieces, the puzzle possibilites are endless!

About Us

Founder Taylor Dalton actually got the idea for Trading Pieces while in quaratine. Taylor found that being trapped inside meant she was piecing through puzzles at a rapid rate. However, money doesn't grow on trees and puzzles can get expensive. Her love of puzzles (and desire to save money) sparked this puzzle subscription design and thus, Trading Pieces was born!
